Version: 1.6.0

Workspace Upgrades & Template Library

A refreshed workspace experience with ready-to-use templates for faster content flow.

Feature list

  • New layout for faster navigation and focus

  • Browse & use pre-built templates for blogs, bios, and emails

  • Pin favorite tools and shortcuts to sidebar

  • A11Y: Sidebar now fully navigable with keyboard

Template Library Launch
Quickly start with high-converting templates for blogs, product descriptions, landing pages, and email campaigns. Each one is optimized for SEO and tone.

Smarter Workspace Navigation
A redesigned left panel makes it easier to jump between tools, saved work, and integrations. Recently opened projects are now listed at the top.

Custom Shortcuts & Pins
Pin your most-used tools like Image Generator, Grammar Fix, or Blog Writer to the sidebar for one-click access. Fully customizable per user.

Apr 4, 2025

Version: 1.5.0

Enhanced Image Generation & SEO Tools

Smarter visuals and improved SEO tools help streamline your content workflow.

Feature list

  • Image prompts now generate high-res assets with better style control

  • SEO Assistant suggests meta tags and optimized headlines

  • Content blocks now support structured data hints

  • A11Y: Image tool now fully keyboard accessible

Generate Smart Visuals
Use natural prompts to create unique images directly inside your editor. Geni AI’s new visual tool offers multiple styles and resolutions—perfect for blog posts or landing pages. You can also replace placeholders with branded visuals on the fly.

Better SEO, Faster
New tools auto-suggest meta titles, descriptions, and headings based on your content type. Whether you're writing a blog or product page, your content gets structured right for ranking.

Optimized Output
Generated content now includes cleaner code and better hierarchy. Exported pages are lighter, scan faster, and are easier to index.

Version: 1.4.0

Slack & Google Drive Integrations

Share content and collaborate across platforms in just one click.

Feature list

  • Share blocks directly to Slack channels

  • Auto-save documents and media to Google Drive

  • Set default folders for content types

  • A11Y: Improved focus states in integration panel

Send to Slack Instantly

Seamless Google Drive Sync
Save every output—docs, images, or content blocks—to the right folder. Set folder rules for blogs, client work, or assets, and Geni AI handles the rest.

Smooth Workflow
Teammates with access can now edit or comment via shared links, all while preserving version control inside your workspace.

Version: 1.3.0

Multi-Language Support & Grammar Boost

Geni AI now speaks your language—with cleaner, sharper grammar fixes.

Feature list

  • Now supports French, Spanish, and German

  • Grammar assistant improves clarity and tone

  • Language toggle per document

  • A11Y: Language switcher includes screen reader support

Smarter Grammar Engine

Per-Project Settings
Set a preferred language once—Geni AI will adapt tone, structure, and spellcheck rules accordingly.
